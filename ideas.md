# Campo Real AGM 2026 — Design Ideas

## Option A — "Verdant Governance"
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Portuguese civic modernism
**Core Principles:** Earthy warmth meets institutional clarity; generous whitespace; strong typographic hierarchy
**Color Philosophy:** Deep forest green (#1B4332) as primary, warm off-white (#FAF8F3) background, terracotta amber (#D97706) as accent — echoing the resort's lush landscape and terracotta rooftops
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with parallax, then alternating asymmetric sections (text-left/image-right, image-left/text-right)
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rules with dot separators; numbered stat callouts in oversized type
**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered fade-ins; sticky nav that compresses on scroll
**Animation:** Subtle entrance animations using framer-motion; smooth language toggle crossfade
**Typography System:** Playfair Display (headings) + DM Sans (body)

## Option B — "Bright Civic" ← CHOSEN
**Design Movement:** Modern civic/NGO transparency aesthetic
**Core Principles:** Clean whites + bold teal; high contrast; data-forward; trustworthy
**Color Philosophy:** Bright white (#FFFFFF) background, deep teal (#00897B) primary, warm slate (#1E293B) for text, light mint (#E0F2F1) for section backgrounds — professional, bright, trustworthy
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero → single-column sections with max-width content, cards in responsive grids
**Signature Elements:** Teal left-border accent cards; large numbered section labels; pill-style language toggle
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover lifts on cards; animated counter stats; smooth scroll anchors
**Animation:** Staggered card entrance on scroll; language toggle with fade transition
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings, bold) + Inter (body) — modern, geometric, readable

## Option C — "Azulejo Modern"
**Design Movement:** Portuguese tile-inspired geometric modernism
**Core Principles:** Bold geometry; cultural reference; high contrast; editorial
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0F172A) with bright cobalt (#2563EB) accents and white — inspired by Portuguese azulejo tiles
**Layout Paradigm:** Masonry-style cards; geometric dividers; full-bleed dark sections alternating with white
**Signature Elements:** Geometric tile-pattern SVG backgrounds; bold all-caps section labels
**Interaction Philosophy:** Dramatic hover states; tile-flip card animations
**Animation:** Bold entrance animations; geometric reveal transitions
**Typography System:** Syne (headings) + Outfit (body)
